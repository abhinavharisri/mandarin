Crafted for Your Comfort

What We Offer

Amenities Designed for Your Stay

Every amenity at Mandarin Orchid has been thoughtfully selected — not for show, but for genuine comfort. From the moment you arrive to the moment you leave, each detail serves your wellbeing.

Free Wi-Fi

Complimentary high-speed internet access available throughout all rooms and common areas. Stay connected without interruption across the entire property.

Room Service

In-room dining available for all guests. Whether a quiet breakfast or a late-night meal, our team brings it to your door with care and promptness.

Daily Housekeeping

Your room is refreshed daily by our attentive housekeeping team. Fresh linens, spotless bathrooms, and an immaculate space — every single day.

Free Parking

Dedicated on-site parking spaces for every villa. Secure, convenient, and complimentary. Arrive by car without any concern for your vehicle.

In-Room Tea & Coffee

Wake to a perfectly brewed cup of Nilgiri tea or coffee, placed thoughtfully in your room. The mountains taste best from your own private space.

Private Entrance

Each villa features its own exclusive entrance — complete privacy from arrival to departure. Your space is yours alone.

Kids' Play Area

A safe, dedicated space for the little ones. Children can explore and play freely while parents relax — a resort that truly welcomes families.

CCTV Security

Your safety is our priority. The property is equipped with comprehensive CCTV surveillance and trained security personnel around the clock.

24/7 Front Desk

Our reception is staffed around the clock. Assistance, recommendations, or simply a warm conversation — we are always here when you need us.

Villa Exclusives

Beyond the Room — Your Villa

Private Gardens

Each villa opens onto a private garden of approximately 2,000 sq. ft. — your own patch of Nilgiri greenery, screened from the world.

Fireplace & BBQ

Evenings around a firepit. Lazy Sunday barbecues. Each villa comes with an outdoor fireplace pit and barbecue grill — made for lingering.

Nilgiri-View Balconies

Select villas offer private balconies that frame the Nilgiri hills like a living painting — the finest way to begin or end a day.

Two Sitting Rooms

Space to spread out, gather, or be alone. Each villa includes two separate sitting areas — generous room for families and groups alike.
