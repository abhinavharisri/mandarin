Frequently Asked Questions

Need Answers?

Everything You Need to Know

Can't find what you're looking for? Contact us directly — we're always happy to help.

What are the check-in and check-out times?

Check-in generally begins in the early afternoon, and check-out is scheduled for late morning. Specific timings follow property policy. Early check-in and late check-out may be arranged subject to availability — additional charges may apply. Please contact us in advance to make arrangements.

Is Wi-Fi available throughout the property?

Yes. Complimentary high-speed Wi-Fi is available for all guests in their rooms as well as all common areas across the property. The connection is reliable and suitable for both leisure browsing and remote work.

Is parking available at the resort?

On-site parking is available at the resort. Each villa has dedicated parking space. Charges and space availability may vary based on the property policy. Please inform us in advance if you are arriving with multiple vehicles.

What amenities are included with the stay?

Guests enjoy complimentary access to free Wi-Fi, air-conditioned rooms, hot water, free parking, 24-hour front desk service, daily housekeeping, CCTV security, room service, in-room tea and coffee, and private entrance. Property facilities include a steam and sauna, mini gym, library, and kids' play area.

Is breakfast included in the stay?

Breakfast availability depends on the booking plan selected. Some plans include breakfast, while others offer it as an add-on. Please check your booking confirmation for details, or contact us at the time of booking to enquire about meal plan options.

Do you offer airport pickup and drop services?

Yes. Airport pickup and drop services are available on request. Additional charges apply based on distance, time, and transport type. Please inform us at least 24 hours in advance so we can make arrangements for you.

Who is the resort suitable for?

Mandarin Orchid is designed to welcome everyone — families, couples, solo travellers, and corporate guests. Our villa layout makes us especially suited for families and groups, as each villa provides private space while shared outdoor areas allow for gathering together comfortably.

Are pets allowed at the resort?

Pet allowance is subject to the property's current pet policy. Please contact us directly before booking if you intend to bring a pet, so we can advise you accordingly and make any necessary arrangements.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept credit cards, debit cards, net banking, and cash. Digital wallet options may also be available. We also offer flexible pay-at-hotel arrangements — you can book now and pay at check-in, subject to our booking terms.

What is the cancellation policy?

Cancellations made 48+ hours before check-in qualify for a full refund (minus processing fees). Cancellations 24–48 hours before incur a 50% charge. Cancellations within 24 hours or no-shows are non-refundable. Please refer to our full Refund Policy for complete details.

Are group and corporate bookings available?

Yes. We welcome group bookings and corporate stay arrangements. Special pricing may be offered depending on group size, duration, and requirements. Please contact us directly to discuss your needs and we will be happy to tailor an arrangement for you.

What safety measures are in place?

Guest safety is a priority at Mandarin Orchid. The property is equipped with comprehensive CCTV surveillance, trained staff, secure access controls, and standard safety protocols throughout. Our 24-hour front desk ensures assistance is always available.

Are there restaurants and markets nearby?

Yes. Kotagiri town is within easy reach and offers restaurants, local markets, tourist attractions, and public transport. The resort itself also provides in-room dining through room service and has dining areas for group meals.

Can I request early check-in or late check-out?

Early check-in and late check-out requests are accommodated subject to room availability. Please contact us in advance to check availability. Additional charges may apply for extended timings.

How do I submit proof of payment?

After booking, please submit proof of payment within 48 hours using your reservation reference number. Failure to do so may result in cancellation of the reservation. You can send it via email to mandarinorchidooty@gmail.com or via WhatsApp.
