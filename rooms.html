Comfortable Rooms for Every Traveller

Each of our sixteen rooms is a carefully considered space — warm wooden interiors, white linen, and a connection to the hills just beyond your window. Two distinct room types, both exceptional.

Room Type I

Deluxe Double Room

A beautifully appointed room that distils the essence of Nilgiri living. Rich teak panelling runs from floor to ceiling, while crisp white linen invites rest. The room is generous, private, and perfectly equipped for a couple or solo traveller seeking comfort without complexity.

Heritage Villa Room
